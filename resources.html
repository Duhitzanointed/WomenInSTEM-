Resources

We strongly believe that there's enough space for everyone in the STEM field and girls who have an interest in STEM deserve to have opportunites regardless of financial background. Down below are some great programs/organizations that are free of charge and will help immensely!

Kode With Klossy

Kode With Klossy is a special program started by a famous model named Karlie Kloss. The program helps girls in high school learn about computer programming and technology. It offers free two-week coding camps in the summer, where the girls can learn how to make websites, apps, and other computer-related things. The goal is to encourage more girls to get involved in the tech industry. The program also connects the girls with successful women who work in tech to provide guidance and support. Kode With Klossy has been very successful in inspiring and empowering young girls to explore the world of coding and pursue careers in technology. Click on the picure for more information!

Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is an organization that helps girls learn how to code. They want to make sure more girls have the chance to be a part of the tech industry. They offer programs like summer camps and clubs where girls can learn about computer programming and other cool tech stuff. The goal is to bridge the gender gap in technology by encouraging and empowering girls to pursue careers in this field. Girls Who Code has been very successful in giving girls the skills and confidence they need to excel in the world of coding and computer science. Click on the picure for more information!

Girls In Tech

Girls in Tech is an organization that supports and promotes women in the technology industry. They want to make sure more women have opportunities and succeed in tech-related careers. They offer various programs, workshops, and events where women can learn new skills, connect with others in the industry, and get support to advance in their careers. The goal of Girls in Tech is to create a more diverse and inclusive tech community where women can thrive and make significant contributions. Overall, Girls in Tech is helping to break barriers and empower women to be successful in the world of technology. Click on the picure for more information!

Code First Girls

Code First Girls is an organization that teaches women how to code. They offer free coding courses and workshops to help women learn new tech skills. The aim is to increase the number of women in the technology industry and give them the confidence to succeed in this field. Code First Girls provides a supportive and friendly environment where women can learn at their own pace and connect with others who share similar interests. It's all about empowering women to thrive in the world of coding and technology. Click on the picure for more information!

Career Girls

"Career Girls" is an educational platform that aims to inspire and empower young girls and women to pursue fulfilling careers and break gender stereotypes. The organization provides online resources, videos, and interviews with successful women from various fields, showcasing their journeys and experiences. The platform seeks to encourage girls to explore diverse career options, gain confidence, and foster leadership skills, ultimately helping them to achieve their professional aspirations.
